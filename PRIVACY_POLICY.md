# Privacy Policy for Hoops Imposter

**Last Updated: December 2, 2024**

## Overview

Hoops Imposter ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Player Names**: When you choose to enter custom player names, these are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers.

### Information Collected Automatically
The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect:
- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Device type and operating system
- App interactions and usage data
- Ad interaction data

## How We Use Information

### Local Data
- Player names and game preferences are stored locally on your device using shared preferences
- This data is used solely to enhance your gaming experience
- We do not transmit this data to any servers

### Third-Party Services
The App uses Google AdMob for advertising purposes. AdMob uses the collected information to:
- Serve personalized advertisements
- Measure ad performance
- Prevent fraud and abuse

## Data Sharing

We do not collect or share any personal information directly. However:
- **Google AdMob**: May collect and share data as described in their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes

## Children's Privacy

The App is rated for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete such information.

## Data Retention

- Local data (player names, preferences) remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data
- AdMob data retention is governed by Google's policies

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access and delete local data by clearing the app's data in your device settings
- Opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings:
  - **Android**: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  - **iOS**: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
